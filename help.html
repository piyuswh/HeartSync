Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I create an account?
    To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the instructions to fill out the registration form.
  • How can I edit my profile?
    After logging in, go to your profile page and click on the "Edit Profile" button. You can then update your information and preferences.
  • How do I upload photos?
    When editing your profile, there will be an option to upload photos. Click on it, select the photos you want to upload from your device, and then save your changes.
  • How can I contact other users?
    You can send messages to other users by visiting their profiles and clicking on the "Send Message" button.
  • How do I report a user?
    If you encounter any suspicious or inappropriate behavior, you can report the user by clicking on the "Report User" button on their profile page.
